The essential reading list

TMS

Multicenter RCTs

  • (O’Reardon et al. 2007): Multicenter study leading to the FDA approval of the “NeuroStar TMS Therapy system”
  • (Herwig et al. 2007): Antidepressant effects of augmentative TMS
  • (Levkovitz et al. 2015): Multicenter trial on deep TMS
  • (Daniel M. Blumberger et al. 2018): THREE-D: Theta Burst is non-inferior to 10-Hz rTMS
  • (Daniel M. Blumberger et al. 2022): FOUR-D: “THREE-D for older patients”
  • (Papakostas et al. 2024): Effectiveness of augmentation with aripiprazole or rTMS versus switching to venlafaxine/duloxetine for treatment-resistant depression

Influential single center RCTs

  • (Pascual-Leone et al. 1996): Rapid stimulation of the left prefrontal cortex.
  • (E. J. Cole et al. 2022): The “SAINT” paper: accelerated TBS
  • (J. Cole et al. 2022): TBS + Cycloserin
  • (Morriss et al. 2024): Connectivity-guided iTBS (DLPFC <-> AI) vs rTMS

Safety

  • (Rossi et al. 2021): Safety and recommendations for TMS use in healthy subjects and patient populations
