The essential reading list

TMS

Neuroscientific background

  • (Barker, Jalinous, and Freeston 1985): Non-Invasive Magnetic Stimulation Of Human Motor Cortex
  • (Siebner et al. 2022): Transcranial magnetic stimulation of the brain: What is stimulated?
  • (Elbau et al. 2023): Funtional Connectivity Mapping for rTMS Target selection in Depression

Clinical trials

Multicenter RCTs

  • (O’Reardon et al. 2007): Multicenter study leading to the FDA approval of the “NeuroStar TMS Therapy system”
  • (Herwig et al. 2007): Antidepressant effects of augmentative TMS (?)
  • (Levkovitz et al. 2015): Multicenter trial on “deep” TMS
  • (Daniel M. Blumberger et al. 2018): THREE-D: Theta Burst is non-inferior to 10-Hz rTMS
  • (Yesavage et al. 2018): Effect of rTMS on Treatment-Resistant Major Depression in US Veterans
  • (Daniel M. Blumberger et al. 2022): FOUR-D: “THREE-D for older patients”
  • (Papakostas et al. 2024): Effectiveness of augmentation with aripiprazole or rTMS versus switching to venlafaxine/duloxetine for treatment-resistant depression

Influential single center RCTs

  • (Pascual-Leone et al. 1996): Rapid stimulation of the left prefrontal cortex.
  • (E. J. Cole et al. 2022): The “SAINT” paper: accelerated TBS
  • (J. Cole et al. 2022): TBS + Cycloserin
  • (Morriss et al. 2024): Connectivity-guided iTBS (DLPFC <-> AI) vs rTMS

Safety & Best practice guidelines

  • (Rossi et al. 2021): Safety and recommendations for TMS use in healthy subjects and patient populations

tDCS

Neuroscientific background

  • (Nitsche and Paulus 2000): Excitability Changes Induced in the Human Motor Cortex by Weak Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation
  • (Liu et al. 2018): Immediate neurophysiological effects of tDCS

Clinical trials

Multicenter RCTs

  • (Loo et al. 2018): tDCS in depression (unipolar and bipolar)
  • (Aust et al. 2022): tDCS combined with group psychotherapy
  • (Burkhardt et al. 2023): tDCS as an additional treatment to SSRIs
  • (Borrione et al. 2024): Home-use tDCS & a digital psychological intervention
  • (Woodham et al. 2024): Home-use tDCS in unipolar depression

Influential single center RCTs

  • (Brunoni et al. 2013): tDCS vs. Sertraline
  • (Brunoni et al. 2017): tDCS vs. Escitalopram

Safety & Best practice guidelines

  • (Antal et al. 2017): Low intensity transcranial electric stimulation: Safety, ethical, legal regulatory and application guidelines

Depression

Measurement

  • (Fried, Flake, and Robinaugh 2022): Revisiting the theoretical and methodological foundations of depression measurement
  • (Zwart, Jeronimus, and Jonge 2018): Empirical evidence for definitions of episode, remission, recovery, relapse and recurrence in depression
