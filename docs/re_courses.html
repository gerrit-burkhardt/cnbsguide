Useful courses & tutorials

TMS

  • The NIMH video series on TMS. Introductory lectures on TMS physics, devices, protocols etc.

Scientific writing

  • The Stanford Writing in the Sciences course. This course teaches scientists to become more effective writers, using practical examples and exercises. Accessible with a free Coursera account.
